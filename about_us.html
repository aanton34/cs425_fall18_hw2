John Doe is a 35 years old landscape photographer based in Cyprus. John's pursuit of landscape photographs have taken him around the world for almost 14 years now. His passion is to show the Nature and its many moods, a passion that is showned through his work and it has attracted fans from all over the world.

John's talent for amazing captures of Nature have made his portfolio epic and extraordinary. His success derives from his patience and his persistent pursuit of all the moments that are consisted from wilderness, and exceptional weather phenomenos.

His photographs have been published extensively worldwide in a large variety of media and he has reached the number of 500 clients, worldwide.

He is a full-time professional from 2004, and he has been recognized for his creativity and his innovative ideas in the modern age of landscape photography. John is often described as one of the most successful landscape photographers of his era.
